Orange Chicken

Orange chicken paired with rice and scallions.

Ingredients

  • 1 Orange
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili-garlic paste
  • Brown sugar
  • Garlic
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Water
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Roast the chicken pieces until they're cooked through
  2. Mix all of the ingredients together to make a thick sauce
  3. Toss the chicken in the orange chicken sauce
